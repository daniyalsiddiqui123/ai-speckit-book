This chapter focuses on the design and implementation of intuitive and effective interaction methods between humans and robots.

<ul>

<p>Upon completion of this chapter, you will be able to:</p>

    <li>Discuss factors influencing trust and acceptance of robots.</li>
    <li>Explore the principles of collaborative robotics.</li>
    <li>Analyze ethical challenges in human-robot interaction.</li>
</ul>
