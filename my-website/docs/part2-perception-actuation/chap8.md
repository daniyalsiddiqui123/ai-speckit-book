# Chapter 8: Robot Locomotion

This chapter explores the various methods by which robots move, covering different locomotion mechanisms and their applications.

<h2>Key Concepts</h2>

<ul>
    <li>Wheeled Locomotion</li>
    <li>Legged Locomotion (e.g., Bipedal, Quadrupedal)</li>
    <li>Tracked Locomotion</li>
    <li>Aerial and Underwater Locomotion</li>
    <li>Gait Generation and Stability</li>
</ul>

<h2>Learning Objectives</h2>

<p>Upon completion of this chapter, you will be able to:</p>

<ul>
    <li>Differentiate between various types of robot locomotion.</li>
    <li>Understand the principles behind wheeled and legged robot movement.</li>
    <li>Analyze the advantages and disadvantages of different locomotion methods.</li>
    <li>Grasp the concepts of gait generation and stability in multi-legged robots.</li>
</ul>
