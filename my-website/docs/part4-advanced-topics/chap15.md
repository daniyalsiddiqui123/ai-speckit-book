# Chapter 15: Ethical and Societal Impact of Robotics

This chapter critically examines the ethical considerations and broader societal implications of advanced robotics and AI.

<h2>Key Concepts</h2>

<ul>
    <li>Robotics and Employment</li>
    <li>Safety and Accountability</li>
    <li>Privacy and Data Security</li>
    <li>Autonomous Decision-Making and Morality</li>
    <li>Regulation and Governance of AI and Robotics</li>
</ul>

<h2>Learning Objectives</h2>

<p>Upon completion of this chapter, you will be able to:</p>

<ul>
    <li>Discuss the impact of robotics on the future of work and employment.</li>
    <li>Analyze safety and accountability issues in autonomous systems.</li>
    <li>Examine privacy and data security challenges posed by robots.</li>
    <li>Engage in debates about autonomous decision-making and robotic morality.</li>
    <li>Understand the emerging regulatory landscape for AI and robotics.</li>
</ul>
