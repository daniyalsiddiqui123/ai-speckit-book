# Chapter 3: Robot Dynamics

This chapter introduces the principles of robot dynamics, focusing on the forces and torques that cause robot motion.

## Key Concepts

*   Newton-Euler Formulation
*   Lagrangian Dynamics
*   Robot Manipulator Equations of Motion
*   Dynamic Control

## Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this chapter, you will be able to:

*   Apply Newton-Euler and Lagrangian methods to derive robot dynamic equations.
*   Understand the forces and torques involved in robot movement.
*   Analyze the dynamic behavior of robotic systems.
*   Explore basic concepts of dynamic control.
