# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the "Teaching Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Course." This course is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the foundational concepts, perception, actuation, intelligence, and advanced topics in the exciting field of physical AI and humanoid robotics.

## Course Overview

This course is structured into four main parts:

*   **Part 1: Foundations of Robotics**: Introduces the basic principles and historical context of robotics, including kinematics, dynamics, and control systems.
*   **Part 2: Perception and Actuation**: Explores how robots perceive their environment through sensors and interact with it through various actuation mechanisms.
*   **Part 3: Intelligence and Learning**: Delves into the artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques applied to robotic systems, including planning, decision-making, and reinforcement learning.
*   **Part 4: Advanced Topics in Robotics**: Covers cutting-edge research and emerging trends in humanoid robotics, human-robot interaction, and ethical considerations.
